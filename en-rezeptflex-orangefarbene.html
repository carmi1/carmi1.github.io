Home Recipe - Orange Coloured Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp canola oil
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 3-4 carrots
  • 300g pumpkin
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 150g orange lentils
  • 2 Litres vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp paprika

Preparation

  1. Pour the canola oil into the pot, and start the heat.
  2. Add potatoes, carrots and pumpkin and then salt and pepper.
  3. Sauté, stirring occasionally for 2-3 minutes
  4. Add lentils, paprika and vegetable stock and stir.
  5. Cook for 45 minutes.
  6. Purée with an immersion blender until smooth.

Das ist alles. Mit Suppenmandeln oder Croutons servieren

This is a bright, healthy, and creamy soup that the girls really enjoy. It's also a customer favorite and relatively easy to make.

Naturally, there are occasional arguments over who gets to use the immersion blender (Stabmixer in German)
