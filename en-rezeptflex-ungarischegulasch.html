Home Recipe - Hungarian Goulash

Ingredients

  • 200g textured vegetable protein
  • 3 Tbsp canola oil
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 peppers
  • 3-4 tsp minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 + 1 Litres vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp yeast extract
  • 3 Tbsp sweet paprika
  • 1 Tbsp smoked paprika
  • Splash red wine

Preparation

  1. Soak the vegetable protein in tap water in a medium-sized pot for 15 minutes
  2. Add the canola oil and the onion to the pot and fry.
  3. Add paprika, garlic salt and pepper. Sauté for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Drain the vegetable protein, then add 1 litre of vegetable stock to the protein and cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the tomatoes and potatoes to the soup pot.
  6. Drain the protein and add it to the pot as well.
  7. Add carrots and both paprikas and stir.
  8. Now add the vegetable stock, yeast extract and bay leaves and cook for 45 minutes.
  9. Add a dash of red wine and mix.

That's all. Serve with good bread.

This is a warm and hearty soup. It conveys the feeling of goulash perfectly. Not easy, but worth the effort.

Best for comfort on a cold winter day.
