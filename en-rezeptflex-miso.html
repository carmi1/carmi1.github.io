Home Recipe - Japanese Miso Soup

Ingredients

  • 100 g Rice noodles
  • 3 Tbsp Canola oil
  • 1 Sheet of Kombu
  • 2 litres tap water
  • 5 sliced Shiitake mushrooms
  • 300g sliced Tofu
  • 2 Tbsp dried Wakame
  • 4 Tbsp dark Miso paste
  • 2 Tbsp Mirin sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame oil
  • 2 chopped Spring onions

Preparation

  1. Cook the Kombu in tap water in a medium pot until boiling, then discard the kombu.
  2. Add canola oil and tofu to a pan and fry until golden brown.
  3. Place the rice noodles and 2 litres of boiling water in a steel bowl.
  4. Dice the tofu, then add the shiitake mushrooms and the diced tofu to the pot and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn off heat. Add miso paste, mirin sauce, sesame oil, then stir.
  6. Add wakame and stir again.
  7. To serve, place the rice noodles in a bowl and then pour in the soup. Garnish with spring onions.

That's all.

This is a very satisfying soup, both in terms of health and aesthetics.

A perfect example of the wonders of Japanese food culture.
