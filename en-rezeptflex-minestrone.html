Home Recipe - Genoese Minestrone

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large potato
  • 3 carrots
  • 300 g pumpkin
  • 3-5 stalks of celery
  • 2 Tbsp fresh thyme
  • 3-4 tsp minced garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 800g diced tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 Litres vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp yeast extract
  • 1 can white beans
  • Splash dry white wine
  • 250g short pasta - cooked
  • Pesto

Preparation

  1. Put the oliven oil, onion, potato, carotts, pumpkin, celery, thyme, garlic, salt and Pepper in pot sauté for 7-8 minutes.
  2. Add the tomatoes, bayleaf and vegetable stock and cook at a simmer for 35 mMinutes.
  3. Discard the bay leaf.
  4. Add the yeast extract, white beans, white wine and pasta and mix well.
  5. Serve in a bowl with a spoonful of pesto.

That's all.

This was our first professional soup. It has a deep and complex flavor that is very special.

It's worth getting the fresh herbs for this.
